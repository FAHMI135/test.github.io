Products & Services

The scope of delivery of goods through 10 services land, express land, sea, air, instant, sameday, trucking cooler, trucking dry, towing and international that we have prepared and with our motto Quick Pick Up, Fast Delivery, Fast Information, making it easier for our customers in determining the choice of delivery of goods.

  • Land

    Land is a delivery service via land transportation with competitive and cost effective rates for the delivery of goods throughout Indonesia. These services were created to answer customer needs for small and large-scale delivery of goods to areas that are easy and affordable throughout Indonesia with delivery in accordance with the estimated delivery.

  • Sea

    SEA is a service for shipping goods via sea with affordable sea cargo rates, and delivery estimates will be carried out precisely and accurately. Freight forwarding services via sea are the right choice to be able to send goods in large quantities or and have a very heavy burden. We come with a procedure that makes it easy for you to send goods by sea and we will serve all types of goods.

  • City Courier

    CITY COURIER is a pickup and delivery service in one area or intercity (within the city and this service prioritizes the lead time of the pickup and delivery process in a short time span in a matter of instant hours (1-2 hours, sameday (6-8 hours spread over several cities) in Indonesia with fast delivery times.

  • Air

    AIR, which is a freight forwarding service via air, we have now become the official cargo agent of the largest airline in Indonesia. Where our services are more coordinated and reliable by issuing receipts/airway bills on behalf of PT. Indah Logistik. Our air service with estimated performance in accordance with the goods arriving at their destination and for all major cities in Indonesia makes us a trusted cargo for on time delivery.

  • Trucking

    TRUCKING is a service for delivering goods via land using a fleet of trucks between cities and between islands where goods will be sent in large quantities / chartered and to meet the needs of cheaper and faster delivery of goods. Trucking services are more effective in loading goods or furniture, electronic equipment and even moving goods in large quantities.

  • Express

    EXPRESS is a delivery service via land express transportation with competitive rates for shipping goods throughout Indonesia. These services were created to answer customer needs for small and large-scale delivery of goods with faster estimates in a matter of days to affordable areas throughout Indonesia with faster delivery than regular land.

  • Towing

    TOWING is a motorbike and car transportation service using towing within the city of Jakarta as well as between cities and provinces. Serving the delivery of motorbikes inside and outside the city via land access. We really understand your time is very valuable to you. And you find it difficult or dear to take your time to drop off or pick up your motorbike / car. We are here to provide the right solution to provide the right and safe solution for the delivery of your favorite motorbike, car/vehicle to and from your desired destination. We have also provided online services, door to door towing services to simplify the process of sending your motorbike, car/vehicle and provide 24 hour service.

  • International

    INTERNATIONAL is a service for shipping goods between countries via sea and air with affordable sea and air cargo rates, and delivery estimates will be carried out precisely and accurately. Shipping services between countries via sea and air are the right choice to be able to send goods in small or large quantities. We come with procedures that make it easier for you to send goods overseas via sea and air quickly, precisely and safely.

  • Trucking Cooler

    TRUCKING COOLER is a service delivery of goods via land using a fleet of cooler trucks or better known as inter-city to inter-island thermoking trucks where the goods to be sent are in the form of vegetables, fruits or food with a certain temperature in large quantities / charter and to meet the needs of shipping goods which is cheaper and faster. Freight forwarding services with cooler trucks can more effectively load frozen food items in large quantities.
